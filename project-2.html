About BIIT Computer Institute


Barani Institute of Information Technology (BIIT) was established at University of Arid Agriculture after a long and deliberate consideration of the growing needs of software development and the initiative launched by Government of Pakistan. The institute was established as a partnership venture between the University of Arid Agriculture (UAAR) and M/S Resource Organizers and Software Engineers (ROSE) International. The institute operates on self-financed scheme, and is initially concentrating on undergraduate and graduate academic programs.BIIT was established at University of Arid Agriculture after a long and deliberate consideration of the growing needs of software development and the initiative launched by Government of Pakistan. Slogan

Realize Your Potential with Effective Training!

Vision

Our aim is to make every student able to earn while studying. Whether it’s a home based job or some part time job, a

student should be able to cover his all financial needs for his higher studies.

Mission

Professionalism is the key to success. Keeping this in mind, Biit students are kept engaged in different official

and other co-curricular activities and tasks. Students are also guided how-to organize, perform and behave in a

professional environment.

Internships

We encourage our students to do internship with us at MASIA Institute, to gain practical experience for their skills.

With a complete practical approach towards programming we train them to the latest technologies, languages and

frameworks.

Our Success

Most of our students are now self-employed or working in different Govt and Non-Govt organizations for their skills

they earned at BIIT Institute.
